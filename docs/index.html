Frequently Asked Questions

Is X-Proxy really free? ▼

Yes! X-Proxy is completely free and open-source. There are no hidden fees, premium tiers, or subscriptions. All features are available to everyone.

What proxy types are supported? ▼

X-Proxy supports HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS4, and SOCKS5 proxies. All types support authentication with username and password.

Is my data safe? ▼

Absolutely! X-Proxy stores all data locally on your device with encryption. We don't collect any personal information or browsing data.

Can I use PAC scripts? ▼

Yes! X-Proxy has full PAC script support. You can use URLs, upload files, or write custom scripts with our built-in editor.

How do I get support? ▼

You can get help through our GitHub issues page, community discussions, or check our comprehensive documentation.
